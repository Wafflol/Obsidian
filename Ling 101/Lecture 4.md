All languages start as Pidgins

#### Pidgin
- A reduced system, often for trade
- No-one's native language (*key test*)
- not a 'real' language
- very useful, and thus common
##### Tok Pisin
Stripped many sounds
Stripped grammar
	no plurals
	no tense marking
	no 'agreement' (lost -s in *he walks*)
	Examples:
		wanpela pik 'one pig'
		tripela pik 'three pigs'
