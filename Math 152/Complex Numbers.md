Complex Conjugate:
$$\bar{z} = a - bi$$
$$Re\{z\} = \frac{{z+\bar{z}}}{2} = a$$
$$Im\{z\} = \frac{{z-\bar{z}}}{2i} = b$$
Magnitude:
$$z*\bar{z} = \mid z \mid^2 = a^2+b^2$$
Multiplication:
$$z_{1}z_{2} = (a_{1}a_{2}-b_{1}b_{2})+(a_{1}b_{2}+b_{1}a_{2})i$$
Division:
$$\frac{z_{1}}{z_{2}} = \frac{z_{1}}{z_{2}}* \frac{\bar{z_{2}}}{\bar{z_{2}}}$$
